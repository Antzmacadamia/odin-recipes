How to make a Flour Tortilla

Making a flour tortilla is easy. It's a recipe made by 1/3 of the planet. You will need
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/3 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of oil
  • salt and/or seasoning
The above amount of ingredients will make 2 medium tortillas, or 1 large 28cm flour tortilla. Salt is your basic seasoning, however herb mix also works well. Next, follow this procedure.
  1. Mix the dry ingredients, flour and salt or herbs.
  2. Add the oil, and mix in. Next add the water
  3. Knead the dough until its firm and soft.
  4. The ratio of 1 cup to 1/3 cup of water is perfect, however add more water or flour if needed to get a perfect dough.
  5. Let the dough sit for 30 minutes. The longer it sits, up to 2 hours, the better and softer it will be. Test it!
  6. Roll flat and fry in a little oil. Any size you like, and as crispy as you like.
  7. Eat!
    8. And thats the recipe. I encourage you to try milk instead of water, and different herbs.
